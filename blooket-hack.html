Finding Scripts

Click on this link: Github-Blooket-Hacks

Find the blooket game you are playing and then click on the name of it. Inside there will be different cheats for that specific game, click on the one that you want (you can add multiple).

Pasting into the Dev Console

After you have got to your desired cheat click the icon to add to your clipboard (copied to clipboard)

Go back to your blooket page

And whle you're in the game press ctrl + shift + i or go to the three dots at the top right of chrome and (More Tools > Developer Tools)

Then look for console and right click on it and click past. Then click enter and the hack should loade
